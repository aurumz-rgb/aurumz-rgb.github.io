About Me

A Medical Student with a keen interest in Development, Open Access and Animation. I've also found an interest in Research, Evidence synthesis based Research methodologies, Evidence synthesis and open-source Research Softwares.

I also have experience in animation using Adobe Flash/Animate and Adobe After Effects. I'm familiar with Adobe Premiere Pro and Photoshop, and I typically use Adobe Illustrator for creating logos and other graphical elements in my projects.

I mostly work with Python and HTML. I've also caught a massive interest in developmental AI in healthcare fields like Radiology. Since my projects are self-funded, I host them using Streamlit and ShinyApps. Support and Sponsorships made via my Github Sponsors is one of the best ways for supporting my work.
